# deployment app

noun

In versions 10.0.0 and higher of Splunk Enterprise:

A unit of content deployed by the agent management to a group of agents. Deployment apps can be fully developed apps, such as those available in Splunkbase, or they can be simple groups of configurations.

In versions lower than 10.0.0 of Splunk Enterprise:

A unit of content deployed by the deployment server to a group of deployment clients. Deployment apps can be fully developed apps, such as those available in Splunkbase, or they can be simple groups of configurations.
